A Practical National Approach

Children are growing up in a digital environment that evolves faster than most parents were ever taught to navigate. Gaming platforms, messaging apps, livestreams and social media create opportunities — but also real risks.

Parents Online Safety Hub was built in Victoria with a clear intention: establish a practical prevention framework that can be applied consistently across Australia.

Because co-parenting is hard — but it becomes stronger when everyone operates from the same baseline. The same digital boundaries. The same expectations. The same preventative standards.

No Alarmism. No Politics. Just Prevention.
